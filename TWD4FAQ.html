Questions about The Walking Dead Characters

Q: What is Rick Grimes' role in the series?

A: Rick Grimes is the main protagonist of the series and a former sheriff's deputy.

Q: Who is Michonne and what is her backstory?

A: Michonne is a skilled swordswoman who joins the group after the fall of the prison. She has a mysterious past but is revealed to have lost her family and become hardened by the apocalypse.

Q: What is the significance of the phrase "We are the Walking Dead"?

A: This phrase, spoken by Rick Grimes, reflects the theme of survival and the idea that the living survivors are just as much a part of the "walking dead" world as the zombies.
